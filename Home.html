Discipline

This is a valuable attribute which is observed in a school and that practice should be taken to the society as well. We should posses self discipline first and then we can manage and show our fellow students how tobehave with good discipline

Leadership

Leadership effects a vast area which is being lead. Therefore a leader should properly control his territory. The guidance is very much helpful to the team members of this associations .

Management
